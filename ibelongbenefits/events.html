Events

One of the main reasons for I-belong is so that you can have one spot to see all the different events on campus. There are over 2,000 events each semester. We will talk about some of the different events available to you here. There are fitness classes, such as zumba, aerobics, water aerobics, cycling, yoga and more. They also have competitions such as powerlifting. Another group is campus rec, this includes many different sports teams or classes, such as fencing,wrestling, jiu jitsu, hockey, and many more. Another group is outdoor recreation, they have opportunities to go horseback riding and rock climbing, rafting and more. There are wellness workshops that teach so many different ways to be well, physically, emotionally, mentally, spiritually. Societies meet together to help with your major and future careers. The career center gives opportunities to meet with different companies that are hiring. They also put on workshops to teach how to write a resume that stands out and practice interviewing skills. The weekly activities group puts on positive activities every week such as roller skating, country dancing, game night and more. The more you look, the more you will find there is something for everyone. You can search this by what is going on each day, or you can be added to a group that you are interested in and see what events they are putting on.
