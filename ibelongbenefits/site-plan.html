Content

Home page

This page will give a brief description of what my website is for and direct them to the two child sites. Students and employees of BYU-I along with prospective students will come to this site to learn more about the benefits of I-belong and how much they can gain from using I-belong as a student or employee. It will also direct them to the I-belong site and show them the locations where they can learn more. There will be a navigation header and footer which will be on each page.

Images for the Home page

Events

Events: This page will explain how you can find events based on what you are interested in or by looking at a specific date and seeing what is coming up. There are so many different groups on I-belong for anything that you are interested in or to try different activities to find what might interest you. There are so many different departments who put on events that are so beneficial to students, and I-belong is the place to learn about these events. You can learn CPR, QPR, workshops from the counseling and wellness centers to help with stress, anxiety, depression. There is a fitness group, campus rec for sports, outdoor activities such as snowmobiling in the winter and horseback riding in the summer. There are department groups with any events that they put on for students as well as society groups which are so helpful to go along with a students major. There are weekly activities such as roller skating, country dancing, latin dancing, laser tag, karaoke, etc. There is a service group called I-serve. And so many more.

Images for the Events Page

Extras

Extras: This page will show what is included in I-belong that can help take you where you need to go such as the campus map, canvas, academic calendar, the BYU-I housing marketplace where you can check out housing board, streaming devotional and the devotional webpage, a student employment job board, peer mentoring meeting scheduler, schedule a student study room, ticket office website, report a concern in Rexburg for the city department, and on the app there is a place you have an online version of your I-card for those times you forget to bring it with you. I will also share where students and employees can learn more about what is available to them to reach more students.
